## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a modern horror classic that delves into the
depths of human psyche and supernatural terror. The eerie atmosphere of the
secluded hotel, combined with the protagonist's gradual descent into madness,
creates a sense of dread that lingers long after the final page. King's
intricate character development adds layers of complexity to the story, making
the horrors all the more impactful. While some may find certain scenes
disturbing, the slow, steady build-up of tension ultimately pays off in a
chilling and unforgettable climax. 'The Shining' is a must-read for horror
enthusiasts looking for a truly immersive and haunting experience.
