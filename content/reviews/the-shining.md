## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a chilling masterpiece that delves into the
depths of human psyche and supernatural horror. King's intricate character
development draws readers into the Torrance family's harrowing experience at the
Overlook Hotel. The atmospheric tension is palpable, creating a sense of dread
that lingers long after the final page. While the pacing may feel slow to some,
each moment is essential in building the suspense to a fever pitch. The
supernatural elements are both terrifying and mesmerizing, adding layers of
complexity to the narrative. 'The Shining' is a haunting tale of isolation,
madness, and the darkness that lurks within us all. A must-read for horror
enthusiasts seeking a psychological thriller that will leave them sleepless in
the dead of night.
