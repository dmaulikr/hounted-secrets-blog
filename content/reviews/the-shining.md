haunted hotel during the winter. As the father's sanity unravels, the
supernatural forces within the hotel threaten the family's very survival.

## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a masterclass in psychological horror. The
tension builds steadily throughout the story, creating a sense of unease that
lingers long after the book is finished. King's skillful characterization makes
the family's descent into madness feel all too real, drawing the reader into
their harrowing experience. The haunted Overlook Hotel serves as a terrifying
backdrop for the unfolding horror, with its malevolent spirits and eerie
corridors adding to the sense of dread. While the pacing may be slow for some
readers, the payoff is well worth it as the story hurtles towards its terrifying
conclusion. 'The Shining' is a must-read for fans of horror, showcasing King at
the top of his game.
