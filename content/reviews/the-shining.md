manifest itself, Jack's sanity unravels, putting his wife Wendy and son Danny in

## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a chilling masterpiece that delves deep into the
psychological horrors of isolation and madness. King's skillful storytelling and
attention to detail create a palpable sense of dread that lingers long after the
final page. The characters are richly developed, with Jack Torrance's descent
into madness portrayed in a hauntingly realistic manner. The Overlook Hotel
itself becomes a character, its malevolent presence seeping into every corner of
the narrative. While the pacing may drag at times, the slow burn serves to
heighten the suspense, leading to a gripping and unforgettable climax. 'The
Shining' is a classic in the horror genre that continues to terrify and
captivate readers to this day.
