## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a chilling masterpiece of psychological horror
that delves into the depths of human darkness and fear. The eerie setting of the
isolated Overlook Hotel serves as the perfect backdrop for the unfolding terror,
with King expertly building a sense of dread that steadily escalates throughout
the story. Jack Torrance's descent into madness is both fascinating and
terrifying to witness, while Danny's psychic abilities add an extra layer of
intrigue to the narrative. King's writing is nothing short of captivating,
drawing readers into a world where reality and nightmare blur together
seamlessly. 'The Shining' is a must-read for horror fans looking for a truly
haunting and unforgettable tale.
