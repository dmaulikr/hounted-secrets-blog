Overlook Hotel with his family. As the isolation and supernatural forces of the
hotel start to unravel Jack's sanity, his son Danny's psychic abilities become
crucial in uncovering the dark secrets of the building.

## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a chilling masterpiece that delves deep into the
psychological disintegration of its characters. King's intricate storytelling
weaves a tale of terror that slowly builds tension until it reaches a crescendo
of horror. The Overlook Hotel itself becomes a character, oozing malevolence and
seeping into the minds of the Torrance family. Jack's descent into madness is
both terrifying and tragic, while Danny's psychic abilities add an extra layer
of intrigue to the narrative. King masterfully crafts a sense of dread that
lingers long after the final page is turned. 'The Shining' is a must-read for
horror fans, showcasing King's unparalleled ability to evoke fear and unease in
his readers.
