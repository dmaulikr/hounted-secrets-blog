descent into madness while isolated in a haunted hotel during the winter. As
supernatural forces begin to manipulate the father, Jack Torrance, his wife
Wendy and psychic son Danny must confront the evil presence lurking within the
hotel before it tears them apart.

## Full spoiler-free review
Stephen King's 'The Shining' is a winter-cabin masterpiece that delves deep into
the psychological horror of isolation and the darkness that can consume even the
most ordinary of families. The eerie setting of the haunted hotel, coupled with
King's knack for creating palpable tension, makes for a gripping read that will
haunt you long after you've turned the final page. The characters are richly
drawn, adding layers of complexity to the unfolding terror. While the pacing may
feel slow at times, each moment builds towards a climax that is both terrifying
and deeply satisfying. 'The Shining' is a must-read for horror fans looking for
a classic that still retains its power to terrify.
