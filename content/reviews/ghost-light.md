events. When a troupe of actors is cursed by a mysterious ghost light, they must
unravel the dark secrets of the theater before it's too late. As the line
between performance and reality blurs, the boundaries of life and death are
tested in this spine-tingling tale of the supernatural.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' offers a hauntingly atmospheric experience that will appeal to
fans of both ghost stories and theater. The author's vivid descriptions bring

until the final curtain falls.
