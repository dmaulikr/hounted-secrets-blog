In 'Ghost Light', a haunted theater becomes the backdrop for a series of
mysterious events when a new production of a cursed play is underway. As the
cast and crew are plagued by unexplained occurrences, they must confront the
vengeful spirits tied to the theater's dark past. The line between reality and
performance blurs as they struggle to survive the malevolent forces at play.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' delivers a chilling tale that will keep readers on edge with its
blend of supernatural horror and theatrical intrigue. The author excels in
creating a palpable sense of dread, utilizing the theater setting to great
effect in heightening the tension and mystery. While the pacing may drag in
parts, the payoff is worth the wait as the plot unfolds with unexpected twists
and revelations. The characters, though not fully fleshed out, serve their roles
well in driving the narrative forward. Overall, 'Ghost Light' is a haunting read
that will appeal to fans of both ghost stories and the dramatic world of
theater, offering a unique and spine-tingling experience that lingers long after
the final curtain falls.
