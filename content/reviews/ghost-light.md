occurrences when a mysterious ghost light appears on stage. As the cast and crew
struggle to uncover the dark secrets behind the play, they find themselves
facing malevolent spirits and a cursed script that may be more than just
fiction.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' delivers a chilling blend of supernatural horror and theatrical
intrigue that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. The author expertly

journey into the dark side of the theater world.
