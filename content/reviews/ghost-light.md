In 'Ghost Light', a haunted theater becomes the setting for a series of eerie
occurrences as a troupe of actors prepare to put on a cursed play. The spirits
of former performers lurk in the shadows, eager to possess the living and enact
their revenge. As the cast and crew delve deeper into the play's dark history,
they must confront the malevolent forces at play before it's too late.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' is a chilling tale that weaves together the realms of theater and
the supernatural with finesse. The author's vivid descriptions bring the haunted
theater to life, making it a character in its own right. The sense of foreboding
