mysterious apparitions, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs,
leading to a spine-chilling climax that will leave readers questioning what is
truly lurking in the darkness of the stage.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' is a haunting tale that seamlessly merges the world of theater
with the supernatural. The author excels at creating a foreboding atmosphere,
using the backdrop of the old theater to enhance the sense of dread. The

'Ghost Light' to be a captivating read that keeps them guessing until the final
curtain falls.
