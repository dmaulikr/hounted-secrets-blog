In 'Ghost Light', a theater troupe discovers a hidden script that unleashes
malevolent spirits within their production. As the ghostly occurrences escalate,
the cast must confront the dark secrets of the theater's past to unravel the
curse that threatens their lives.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' expertly weaves together the world of theater with chilling
supernatural elements, creating a gripping and atmospheric read. The author's
attention to detail shines through in the vivid descriptions of both the
backstage drama and the spectral hauntings that plague the characters. While the
pacing may lag in some parts, the slow burn serves to heighten the tension
leading to a thrilling climax. The characters are well-crafted and their
interactions add depth to the unfolding mystery. However, some plot twists may
not come as a surprise to seasoned horror readers. Overall, 'Ghost Light' is a
haunting tale that will appeal to fans of both ghost stories and the theatrical
world, offering a perfect blend of suspense and supernatural intrigue.
