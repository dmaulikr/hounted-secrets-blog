In 'Ghost Light,' a renowned theater company is plagued by mysterious
occurrences as they prepare to stage a cursed play. As tensions rise and strange
phenomena intensify, the cast and crew must confront the malevolent forces at
play before it's too late.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' weaves a chilling tale of supernatural occurrences within the
world of theater, blending elements of ghostly folklore with the high-stakes
drama of the stage. The author excels in creating a palpable sense of dread,
utilizing the backdrop of the theater to enhance the story's haunting
atmosphere. While the pacing may drag in places, the slow build-up ultimately
serves to heighten the suspense, leading to a climactic finale that is both
thrilling and satisfying. The characters, though somewhat underdeveloped, serve
as conduits for the eerie events unfolding around them. Fans of ghost stories
and theatrical intrigue will find 'Ghost Light' to be a compelling read that
delivers on its promises of spookiness and suspense.
