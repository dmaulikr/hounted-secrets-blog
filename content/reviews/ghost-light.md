In 'Ghost Light', a theater troupe discovers that a cursed script brings to life
the vengeful spirits of actors who died under mysterious circumstances. As they
try to unravel the dark secrets behind the play, they find themselves trapped in
a sinister world where reality and fiction blur, and the line between life and
death grows thin. With eerie stage-door spirits and a malevolent force lurking
in the shadows, the characters must confront their deepest fears before they
become the next victims of the curse.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' is a haunting tale that immerses readers in the chilling world of
a cursed theater. The author excels at creating a tense atmosphere, with vivid
descriptions that bring the eerie setting to life. The blend of supernatural
elements and theatrical intrigue adds a unique twist to the horror genre,
keeping readers on edge as they navigate the blurred lines between reality and
fiction. While the pacing may drag in some parts, the overall sense of dread and
suspense is well-maintained throughout the story. The characters, though not all
fully fleshed out, serve their roles in unraveling the mysteries of the cursed
script and confronting the malevolent forces at play. Fans of ghostly tales and
theatrical settings will find 'Ghost Light' to be a captivating and spine-
tingling read.
