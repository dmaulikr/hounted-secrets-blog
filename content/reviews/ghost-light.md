lurking in the shadows before opening night brings deadly consequences.

## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' offers a spine-tingling combination of ghostly encounters and
theatrical intrigue that will keep readers on edge. The author skillfully weaves
a haunting atmosphere within the walls of the theater, creating a sense of
foreboding that lingers long after the final curtain falls. The fusion of
supernatural elements with the behind-the-scenes world of stage productions adds
a fresh and captivating layer to the traditional ghost story. While the pacing
may falter occasionally, the chilling suspense and eerie ambiance make up for
any moments of sluggishness. The characters, though not all equally fleshed out,
each contribute to the mounting tension and sense of impending doom. Overall,
'Ghost Light' is a captivating read for fans of both horror and the dramatic
arts, offering a haunting tale that will leave you checking for ghosts in the
shadows long after you've finished reading.
