## Full spoiler-free review
 'Ghost Light' is a spine-tingling tale that blends the allure of the theater
with the dread of the supernatural. The author skillfully creates a haunting
atmosphere that lingers long after the final curtain falls. The characters are

terrifying unknown within the theater. While the pacing dips in the middle, the

Overall, 'Ghost Light' is a captivating read for those who enjoy a blend of
ghostly lore and theatrical drama, offering a satisfying mix of suspense and
supernatural thrills.
